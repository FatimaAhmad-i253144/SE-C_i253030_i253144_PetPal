Why should you feed your dog homemade food?

Homemade diets are becoming more popular because you can control the ingredients, eliminating the risk of dog food recalls. Many people want their dogs to eat healthier, just like them. Homemade diets appeal to some because they use whole foods, often seen as healthier than processed dry or wet dog food.
